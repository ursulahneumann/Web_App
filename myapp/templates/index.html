Female Cholesterol and BMI over Time Worldwide

On the Gapminder website I found two datasets, one for female BMI over time (“Body Mass Index (BMI), women, Kg/m2”) and one for total cholesterol levels over time (“Cholesterol (fat) in blood, women, mmol/L”). These datasets are provided by the MRC-HPA Center for Environment and Health and span the years 1980 - 2008 for 199 countries. I chose to investigate the top 10 economies in the world
